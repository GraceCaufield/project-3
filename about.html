About Me

My Name is Grace Caufield. I am a senior at the University of Monatana. I am majoring in art with a minor in media arts. I have an expected graduation date of December 2024. I am originally from Livermore, California.

I started making art when I was a freshman in high school. I originally started out as a painter, and have since learned a variety of different styles and mediums. I am now looking to pursue a career in digital art, illustration, writing and animation.

In my free time, you can find me reading, writing, watching cartoons, or teaching art lessons to elementary school students. And, of course, drawing!

Experience

I have designed a shirt logo for Veeva Systems, a medical software company.

I was commissioned by the University of Monatana Forestry Department to design a poster for the 2024 Forester's Ball.

I have experience in writing, both in the style of television and film scripts.
